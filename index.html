The Data

Summary

Our data shows that global temperature (degrees Celsius) has been steadily increasing, resulting in a decrease in arctic sea ice levels (millions of squarekilometers) and an increase in global sea levels (millimeters). These visualizations prove the increase in sea levels and decrease in arctic ice are correlated to the rise in global temperature.
